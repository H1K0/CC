Описание

As you can see, I'm really addicted to anime music. :D

So, this is a new anime project by me. I called it To The Sky, cuz I imagined a... well, a sky view. Again happy consonant melodies, again soft harmonies and sound design... As I like it all. Classical H1K0 style, I think.

As you can see, it's a short loop with the length of exactly 2 minutes. How did I manage to do it so exactly? Don't know, actually. It happened just suddenly. It's just because of the tempo in it (128 BPM) and 64 bars length.

As it always is, you can freely use it in your noncommercial projects, just credit me as an author of the music. If you wanna use it in a commercial project, contact me and we'll discuss all the details.
