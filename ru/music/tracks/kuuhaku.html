Описание

Japanese word 空白 (kuuhaku) means empty[ness], blank. So this track is a journey to explore emptyness.

In math, emptyness is signed as zero. And the interesting fact is that zero is divided by all numbers. In other words, NOTHING is divided by EVERYTHING. So we can divide it into infinitely small pieces. And it always results in zero itself.

However, EVERYTHING signed as infinity has the same characteristic. It also divides by all numbers, resulting with itself.

Everything is nothing and nothing is everything?..

Well, just kidding. This track is just my first track in 2019 made using only Sytrus plugin. :D
