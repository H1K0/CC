Описание

Yay, one more only Sytrus track!

It all started when I was playing the piano in the evening. After lots of improvisations a melody came in my brain. I'd tried it in many keys, variations and tempos before it became the first two melodies in this track.

As you can see, I haven't made anything for a long time but that's why I was working hard on my musicmaker skills all along the summer. I'm still not happy with this but, anyway, here's the result which I'm really proud of.
