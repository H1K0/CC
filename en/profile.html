Yay, hello!

My full name is Masahiko AMANO. As seen, the name is Japanese and in original spells 天人楽彦. However, this name seems to be rather long to write or pronounce so if lazy just call me my nickname H1K0 (pronouncing hiko).

First of all, I'm a musician. From the age of 3 or 4 I often played on my mom's old synthesizer, then at the age of 6 I attended into a music school and at the age of 7 I composed my first piece — a simple piano waltz. At the age of 10 I started playing the guitar and trying to compose pop songs. Then, I graduated with honors from music school at the age of 13, knew about DAW and started making electronic music. Three years later, that's when I was 16, my friend and I formed the duo ZingerS and the problem of lyrics seemed to be almost solved for me. A few more time passed, and shortly before my eighteenth birthday it was made very clear for me that for almost all the previous time I'd been just marking time. So, since then I began to try myself in other various genres and styles, moreover I also started studying mixing and mastering.

Also I'm a photographer since the age of 17.
