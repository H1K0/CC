Description

I got tired of breakbeat so decided to make something in classical genre. Here I am, this is an organ (which I love to play) and kinda orchestral cantata. I used strings, something like a flute (but as I think it sounds like a harmonica) and piano in the end. Best in it I like the ending, when it becomes A-dur and when the piano appears. That sounds almost like a full orchestra! When I listen it at night I see the sky, but I imagine it with brighter stars and with the moon. That's actually what the name of this means.
