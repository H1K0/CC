Description

I got an idea for this when I was riding a bicycle around the village. There're huge fields and they makes very romantic view. I haven't got a girlfriend yet, but one moment I imagined myself meeting with an abstract girl on these fields... So when I got to my computer I let out almost all my emotions (as I could) in this one.
