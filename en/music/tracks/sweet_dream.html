Description

Yay! I'm making music again! But actually this one is an improvisation. Just sat in front of my computer, had a dream, 2 hours passed and I completed this. Inspired by romantic anime and touching moments in all. I think it would sound good in a film or anime when the two lovers meet again or when someone is telling about his/her life...
